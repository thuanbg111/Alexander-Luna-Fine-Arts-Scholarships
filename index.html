Alexander Luna Fine Arts Scholarships

We provide scholarship monies to students for education purposes. Our awards support young artists in disciplines like visual arts, music, theater, and dance—removing financial barriers so talent can flourish.

Learn more See programs

What We Do

Direct scholarships and creative opportunities for emerging artists.

  • Merit- and need-based scholarships for Fine Arts students
  • Portfolio & materials micro‑grants
  • Mentorship and workshops with practicing artists

Scholarship Awards

Tuition support for students pursuing accredited arts programs.

Portfolio Grants

Small, fast grants for supplies, exhibition fees, and auditions.

Mentorship & Workshops

Learning sessions with artists and alumni to build skills and networks.
